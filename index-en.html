PAINT / WALL COATINGS

Take a tour of our Catalog!

Our paints are formulated with pure acrylic resin combined with styrene or polyurethane resin to enhance adhesion and elasticity. The paint contains fine mineral fillers such as calcium carbonate or titanium dioxide for a smooth surface, excellent coverage, and resistance to color bleeding.
Additional ingredients include anti-mold, anti-alkali, and UV-resistant agents to prevent yellowing, peeling, or fading over time. Water-based formulas are safe for health, free from lead and heavy metals, and suitable for interiors, including spaces for children.
Thanks to advanced technology, the coating offers superior moisture resistance, waterproofing, easy cleaning, stain resistance, and long-lasting color. Besides standard smooth finishes, we also offer special-effect paints such as stone, concrete, velvet, and metallic finishes to add a luxurious and modern touch to your walls.
